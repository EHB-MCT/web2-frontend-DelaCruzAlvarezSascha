Welcome to the Genshin Impact teambuilder

This guide is here to assist you to prepare for the domain called the Spiral Abyss.
The Spiral Abyss is a special type of domain which can be accessed through a wormhole in the sky.
It exists of 2 sections, the first is a series of eight challenges you can tackle as soon as you unlock the Spiral Abyss, and the second is a set of recurring trials.
